How It Works

Two sides. One tap.

Whether you need to fill a spot on tomorrow's crew or you've got an open day to pick up work — CrewFinder connects you in minutes.

I Need a Worker
1

Post what you need

"Need a climber with rigging experience for a 2-day removal. $300/day." Takes 30 seconds.

2

Browse real profiles

See skills, ratings, work photos, insurance docs, and availability. No paperwork. No guesswork.

3

Hire, rate, and rehire

Message and hire on the spot. Rate after the job. Save good ones to your crew list — rebook in one tap.

I Need Work
1

Build your profile

Check off your skills, upload work photos and insurance, set your rate and availability.

2

Browse jobs near you

See pay, company ratings, and job details. Message companies directly and express interest.

3

Work, get rated, build your rep

Every job builds your profile. High ratings = more offers and better pay.

Why CrewFinder

Built different on purpose

We're not Indeed. We're not Craigslist. CrewFinder is the only platform built from the job site up — for the people who actually do the work.

📋

Profiles, Not Resumes

Skills, work photos, ratings, and insurance docs. Companies see what you can do — not a paragraph nobody reads.

⭐

Two-Way Ratings

Companies rate workers. Workers rate companies. Reputation is earned on the job site, not in an interview.

⚡

On-Demand Hiring

Need someone tomorrow? Post it tonight. Workers get notified and can accept on the spot.

💰

Day Rates or Hourly

Every post shows the actual pay. Flat day rates or hourly — you pick. No bait-and-switch.

👥

Crew Roster

Save your best workers. Add notes. Rebook in one tap. Build your go-to crew over time.

🛡️

Insurance Verified

Workers can upload liability insurance. Companies see who's insured at a glance — less risk, more trust.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

What We Collect

When you create a CrewFinder account, we collect your name, email address, and location. If you're a worker, we also collect your skills, certifications, availability, and pay preferences. If you're a business, we collect your company name and description. You may optionally upload work photos and insurance documents.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to operate CrewFinder — matching workers with businesses, enabling messaging, displaying profiles and ratings, and sending notifications about jobs and messages. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Who Sees Your Data

Your profile information (name, skills, ratings, location, availability) is visible to other authenticated CrewFinder users for the purpose of hiring and job matching. Your email address is only visible to you. Messages are private between sender and receiver.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS) with encryption at rest and in transit. We use row-level security policies to ensure users can only access data they're authorized to see.

Cookies & Analytics

CrewFinder uses minimal cookies required for authentication. We may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand how the platform is used. We do not use advertising trackers.

Your Rights

You can update or delete your profile at any time from within the app. If you want your account and all associated data permanently deleted, contact us and we'll process your request within 30 days.

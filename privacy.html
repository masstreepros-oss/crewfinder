Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

CrewFinder ("we," "us," or "our") operates the CrewFinder application and website at crewfinder.pages.dev. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a CrewFinder account, we collect:

  • Name — your full name for your profile
  • Email address — for authentication, notifications, and account recovery
  • Phone number — for in-app voice calling and optional contact
  • Location — city, state, and/or zip code for job matching

Profile Information

  • Work photos — images you upload to showcase your work portfolio
  • Skills and certifications — professional qualifications you list
  • Availability and pay preferences — scheduling and compensation details
  • Company information — business name, description, and logo (for business accounts)

Usage Information

  • Messages — content of in-app messages between users
  • Call logs — metadata about voice calls made through the platform (time, duration, participants)
  • Job posts and responses — listings you create or respond to
  • Ratings and reviews — feedback you leave for other users

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and browser information
  • IP address (for security and approximate geolocation)
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Timestamps of interactions

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Operate and maintain the CrewFinder platform
  • Match workers with businesses based on skills, location, and availability
  • Enable messaging and voice communication between users
  • Display profiles, ratings, and reviews to other authenticated users
  • Send notifications about jobs, messages, and platform updates
  • Process subscription payments and manage billing
  • Improve the platform through anonymized usage analytics
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

Supabase (Database & Authentication)

Stores your account data, profiles, messages, and job listings. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Hosted on AWS infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy

Twilio (Voice Calling)

Powers in-app voice calls. Twilio processes phone numbers and call metadata to connect calls. Twilio Privacy Policy

Stripe (Payment Processing)

Processes subscription payments for business accounts. Stripe collects payment information (credit card details) directly — we only store your subscription status, not payment card details. Stripe Privacy Policy

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display the Outfit typeface. When you load a page, your browser connects to Google's servers, which may log your IP address. Google Privacy Policy

Cloudflare (Hosting & CDN)

Our site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect anonymized analytics data and processes requests through their CDN. Cloudflare Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, we permanently remove all personal data within 30 days. Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics.
  • Messages: Messages are retained as long as both users' accounts are active.
  • Call logs: Call metadata is retained for 90 days, then automatically purged.
  • Payment records: Billing history is retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

CrewFinder uses:

  • Authentication tokens (localStorage) — required for you to stay logged in
  • Cookie consent preference (localStorage) — remembers your consent choice
  • Last sync timestamp (localStorage) — for offline functionality
  • Service Worker cache — for offline access and performance

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal data via your profile settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at masstreepros@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days.

EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Restriction: Request we limit how we process your data
  • Object: Object to data processing based on legitimate interests

Our legal basis for processing your data is: (a) your consent when you create an account, (b) contractual necessity to provide the service, and (c) legitimate interest in platform security and improvement.

7. Children's Privacy

CrewFinder is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at masstreepros@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • Row-level security policies on our database
  • Authentication via secure tokens (Supabase Auth)
  • Input sanitization to prevent injection attacks
  • Regular security audits

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of CrewFinder after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
